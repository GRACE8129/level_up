Fisherman Soup

Fisherman Soup is a delicious Nigerian delicacy native to the riverine regions of Nigeria. It's a spicy and savory soup made with fresh seafood like fish, shrimp, and periwinkle, and often enjoyed with pounded yam or fufu. Perfect for seafood lovers!

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish (Catfish or Tilapia)
  • Prawns or shrimp
  • Periwinkle
  • Palm oil
  • Fresh pepper and onions (blended)
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Scent leaves or uziza leaves
  • Water

Instruction

  1. Wash and clean the fish, prawns, and periwinkle properly.
  2. Season the fish and seafood with salt, seasoning cubes, and a little blended pepper, then set aside to marinate for a few minutes.
  3. In a pot, pour some water and bring to a boil. Add the marinated fish and seafood.
  4. Add the blended pepper and onions, palm oil, and ground crayfish.
  5. Allow it to cook for 10 to 15 minutes or until the fish is properly done.
  6. Add scent leaves or uziza leaves and stir gently to avoid breaking the fish.
  7. Simmer for another 3 to 5 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve hot with pounded yam, fufu, or any preferred swallow.
